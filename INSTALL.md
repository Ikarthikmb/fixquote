# Repair Estimator – Chrome Extension

## How to Install

1. Open Chrome and go to `chrome://extensions`
2. Enable **Developer mode** (toggle in the top-right corner)
3. Click **Load unpacked**
4. Select the `repair-estimator-extension` folder
5. The extension icon appears in your toolbar

## How to Use

### Adding Parts
- Browse any product on **PhoneLCDParts**, **MobileSentrix**, or **eBay**
- Click the blue **Add to Estimate** button (floating, bottom-right of page)
- OR right-click anywhere on the page → **Add to Repair Estimate**
- The button can be dragged to reposition it

### Viewing the Estimate
- Click the extension icon in the toolbar to open the **side panel**
- Parts appear with name, price, and quantity controls
- Adjust quantity with the +/− buttons
- Remove items with ✕

### Labour
- Labour rates for common devices are pre-loaded (iPhone, iPad, Samsung, etc.)
- Check the box next to a device to include its labour in the total
- Edit any rate by clicking the dollar amount field — the new rate is saved automatically
- Click **+ Add Device** to add a new device/rate

### Tax
- Default tax rate is **7%** applied to parts + labour
- Click the tax rate field in the summary to change it

### Export
- **Copy Summary** — copies a plain-text estimate to clipboard
- **Print** — opens a print-ready version in a new tab
