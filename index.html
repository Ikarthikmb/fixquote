Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2024

FixQuote ("we", "our", or "the extension") is a Chrome browser extension that helps repair professionals build estimates. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

1. Information We Collect

We collect only what is necessary to provide the service:

  • Account information — if you create an account, we store your email address and a secure authentication token provided by Firebase Authentication.
  • Estimate data — parts, prices, labour rates, job names, and totals you enter are saved locally on your device and, if you are signed in, synced to your private Firestore document.
  • Support tickets — if you submit a support ticket, the message and your email address are stored in Firestore so we can respond.

We do not collect browsing history, page content beyond what you explicitly add to an estimate, or any financial information.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To save and sync your estimates and job history across your devices.
  • To respond to support requests you submit through the extension.
  • To maintain your session so you do not need to sign in every time.

We do not sell, rent, or share your data with third parties for marketing purposes.

3. Data Storage

Data is stored in two places:

  • Locally — using Chrome's chrome.storage.local API on your device. This data stays on your machine unless you sign in and enable sync.
  • Firebase / Firestore — if you are signed in, your estimates and tickets are synced to a private Firestore document accessible only by you and the extension administrator. Firebase is a Google Cloud service. See Firebase Privacy.

4. Guest Mode

You can use FixQuote without creating an account. In guest mode, all data is stored locally on your device only and is never transmitted to our servers.

5. Permissions

FixQuote requests the following Chrome permissions:

  • storage — to save estimates and settings on your device.
  • activeTab / scripting — to read the product name and price from the current page when you click "Add to Estimate" or "Grab from Page."
  • sidePanel — to display the estimate panel alongside your browser.
  • tabs — to open the full-page view and admin panel.
  • identity — to support Google Sign-In.

We only read page content when you actively interact with the extension on a supported site. We do not monitor your browsing activity passively.

6. Third-Party Services

  • Firebase Authentication — handles sign-in securely. We never see your password.
  • Google Firestore — cloud database for syncing your data.

Both services are operated by Google and subject to Google's privacy policies.

7. Data Retention

Your data is retained as long as your account is active. You can delete your data at any time by contacting us at the email below. Local data can be cleared by removing the extension from Chrome.

8. Children's Privacy

FixQuote is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top will reflect any changes. Continued use of the extension after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
