FixQuote Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

FixQuote ("the extension") is a Chrome extension that helps repair technicians build accurate repair estimates by capturing part prices from supplier websites. This policy explains what data the extension collects, how it is used, and how it is stored.

1. Data We Collect

  • Account credentials – If you create an account, your email address and a hashed password are stored securely via Firebase Authentication (Google LLC). We never see or store your password in plain text.
  • Estimate data – Repair estimates, job history, labour rates, tax settings, and customer names you enter are stored locally on your device and, if you are signed in, synced to your private Firestore database.
  • Product information – When you use "Grab from Page", the extension reads the product name and price visible on the current supplier tab. This data is stored only in your estimate and is never sent to our servers independently.
  • Authentication token – A session token is stored in chrome.storage.local to keep you signed in. It is never shared with third parties.

2. Data We Do Not Collect

  • Browsing history or any data from websites other than the supported supplier pages you actively interact with.
  • Payment or financial information of any kind.
  • Location data.
  • Any data from pages other than supported supplier sites (phonelcdparts.com, mobilesentrix.com, ebay.com).

3. How Data Is Used

All data collected is used solely to provide the core functionality of FixQuote:

  • Generating and saving repair estimates.
  • Syncing your job history and settings across devices when you are signed in.
  • Authenticating your account.

We do not sell, share, or use your data for advertising or analytics.

4. Data Storage and Security

  • Local storage – Estimate data is cached in chrome.storage.local on your device.
  • Cloud storage – If you create an account, data is synced to Google Firestore under your unique user ID. Access is protected by Firestore security rules that allow only your account to read or write your data.
  • Firebase Authentication – Managed by Google Firebase. See Firebase Privacy Policy.

5. Third-Party Services

FixQuote uses the following Google services, subject to Google's Privacy Policy:

  • Firebase Authentication – account creation and sign-in.
  • Cloud Firestore – cloud storage of your estimate data.
  • Google Identity (optional) – Google Sign-In, if you choose to use it.

6. Data Retention and Deletion

Your data is retained as long as your account exists. To delete all cloud data, sign in, then contact us at the email below and we will permanently delete your Firestore records. Local data can be cleared by uninstalling the extension or clearing extension storage in Chrome settings.

7. Children's Privacy

FixQuote is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy occasionally. The "Last updated" date at the top will reflect any changes. Continued use of the extension after an update constitutes acceptance of the new policy.
